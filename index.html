About Us

Welcome to the Pasig City Museum, a vibrant hub dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich history, culture, and heritage of Pasig City. Our museum serves as a bridge between the past and the present, offering visitors an immersive experience into the city's storied legacy.

Our Mission: To educate, inspire, and engage the community through interactive exhibits, artifacts, and stories that highlight Pasig's evolution from a historical settlement to a modern urban center.

What We Offer:

  • Historical exhibits on Pasig's colonial and pre-colonial history
  • Cultural artifacts and artworks from local artists
  • Educational programs for schools and families
  • Special events and workshops

Located in the heart of Pasig, our museum is committed to fostering a deeper appreciation for local history and culture. Join us in exploring the stories that shape our city!

Our Services

At Pasig City Museum, we provide a range of services to enhance your visit and support learning and community engagement.

Guided Tours

Expert-led tours for individuals, groups, and schools. Learn fascinating stories behind our exhibits.

Educational Programs

Workshops, seminars, and interactive sessions tailored for students and educators.

Events and Exhibitions

Special exhibitions, cultural events, and community gatherings throughout the year.

Museum Shop

Books, souvenirs, and memorabilia related to Pasig's history and culture.

Cafeteria

Relax with local cuisine and refreshments during your visit.

Accessibility Services

Wheelchair access, audio guides, and accommodations for visitors with disabilities.
